Impactful Storyteller . Tech Tinkerer . Relentless Foodie

I’m a user experience designer. I have completed my Master’s degree in Interaction Design from National Institute of Design, Bangalore. Prior to joining NID, I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from IIIT-Delhi. My interest lies at the confluence of technology & design. I love exploring new & emerging technologies - to shape impactful experiences around them. Solving complex problems and decoding the burning consumer needs, gives me the opportunity to connect with the most interesting of people around - understand their stories and uncover the most intricate nuances of human behaviour. It perpetually builds my eagerness to humanise technology and make it accessible, one experience at a time.

My strong advocacy for design-led business strategies has helped me balance the business requirements with the user needs, and parallelly sensitise businesses to the voice of its consumers.

In my latest work experience at Samsung, I have worked across Samsung's diverse portfolio - Smartphones, Home Appliances, Smartwatches and new product innovations. With the expertise to deeply understand the socio-cultural / economic / political landscape of consumer markets, along with the feasibility of tech in these markets, I have led global researches and projects to create locally inspired global consumer experiences. I played an active role in projects from inception to commercialization, gaining a holistic understanding of the product lifecycle. I created design-led business strategies to shape impactful consumer experiences, keeping emerging technologies at the core of innovation. Apart from work, I spend time creating motion stories with Processing, capturing moments on camera, and creating fun content from travel trips - which I share on Instagram.
